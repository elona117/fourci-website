About FOURCi

Creating resilient communities across the Sahel

FOURCi (Foundation for Organized Urban Resilience & Community Initiatives) is dedicated to delivering locally-led environmental programs that reduce vulnerability to climate change. Our work covers awareness, restoration, advocacy, and youth empowerment.

Our Programs Get Involved

Partners: Local councils, schools, volunteer groups

Our Approach

We combine education, hands-on restoration, and policy engagement to produce sustainable outcomes that are led by the people who live in the communities we serve.

Community-Led Projects

We prioritize local leadership and culturally-appropriate solutions.

Evidence-Based

Programs are monitored and adapted to ensure measurable impact.

Policy & Advocacy

We build partnerships to scale local solutions into national policy.
